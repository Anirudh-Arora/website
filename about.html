About

Anirudh Arora

I am a final-year Environmental Science major at IISER Bhopal and a visiting researcher at the DST Centre of Excellence in Climate Modeling, IIT Delhi. My work focuses on understanding climate model uncertainty, air pollution dynamics, and sustainable geoengineering interventions.

Through interdisciplinary training, I combine modeling, policy analysis, and field-based ethnography to investigate the climate crisis and help inform just and effective environmental action. I will be joining KAUST in June 2025 for my master's thesis.
